## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter softened
- 1/2 cup honey
- 1/2 cup powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon

## Directions

1. Combine butter, honey, powdered sugar, and cinnamon in a medium sized bowl. Using a hand mixer beat until the ingredients are whipped and combined.
